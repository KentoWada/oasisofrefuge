Background

This is a charity instituted in 2023, an idea birthed by Joshua Dikia and he's associates. With support from the nationals and global aid, it has continued to grow into a more reputable and non-selective unifying bodyencompassing multitudes of the desolate and unfortunate families or individuals incapacitated by life battles. The organisation is channelled to operate in the northern half of the country but in the due course, God-willing, will radiate traversing the entire central, eastern, western and southern parts of the nation with consideration of the countries.

Mission

To inscribe innovativeness, uplift and revive lives of today's youths.

Beneficiaries

The organisation has put up aid packages for the orphans, financially incapable families, talented but incapable children, disabled individuals and many others with related challenges. Everybody is entitled to lead a problem-free life but due to unavoidable circumstances, challenges have become part and parcel of us which need to be encountered leaving no solution but Providence.

Objectives
  • Provide education to all the unable and disable.
  • Promote talents of the youth in various disciplines.
  • Alleviate sufferings and miseries in different families.
  • Transform the youth into creative, competent, self reliant and highly esteemed individuals.
  • Forming a profound and extensive global interaction and network of youths.
Future plans
  • Having more than 80% of the beneficiaries thrive in various fields across the globe.
  • Have a global influence and impact on the lives of many in different nations.
  • Partner with hundreds of thousands of families, bodies and individuals and rescue lives of many.
